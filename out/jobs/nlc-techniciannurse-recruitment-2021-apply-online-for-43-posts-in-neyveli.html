Description

New Central Government Jobs for 10th pass,12th pass, and nursing candidates have been published by Neyveli Lignite Corporation for the vacancies of Technician/Nurse. If you are searching for a government job in Neyveli then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Technician/Nurse, from 11.05.2021, and till 27.05.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Neyveli and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Technician/Nurse vacancies in Neyveli, your 10th,12th, B.Sc Nurse, BPT / MPT must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 11.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 27.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 58  years old.

NLC Technician/Nurse Pay Scale

According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is.

  • Nurse – Rs. 23,500/-
  • Dialysis Technician – Rs. 23,500/-
  • Physiotherapist – Rs. 26,000/-
  • Male Nursing Assistant – Rs.16,500/-
  • Female Nursing Assistant — Rs.16,500/-
  • Emergency Care Technician – Rs. 23,500/

How to apply for NLC Technician/Nurse Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Technician/Nurse. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of NLC  and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by NLC.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
