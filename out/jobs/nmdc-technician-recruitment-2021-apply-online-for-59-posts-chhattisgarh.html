Description

New Central Government Jobs for diploma holders have been published by National Mineral Development Corporation for the vacancies of Technician. If you are searching for a government job in Chhattisgarh then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Technician, from 10.05.2021, and till 15.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for Technician vacancies in Chhattisgarh, your ITI, Diploma, Degree must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 10.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 15.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 37  years old. 

NMDC Technician Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Chhattisgarh Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 10,000 to 20,000/–.

How to apply for NMDC Technician Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Technician. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of NMDC and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by NMDC.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
