Name of the post:

Description:

Bharat Electronics Limited issued a total of 268 vacancies for the posts of Project Engineer. The online application for these posts under BEL shall start from 21.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 05.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Application fee:

The application fee for BEL Project Engineer Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 21.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 05.05.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree in BE/B.Tech  to apply for the post of Project Engineer

Project Engineer Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 32 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

BEL Project Engineer Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Project Engineer is INR 35,000-50,000
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed. 

How to apply for BEL Project Engineer Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of BEL.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Project Engineer.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
