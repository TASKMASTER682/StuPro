Name of the post:

  • Academic Associate

Description:

Indian Institute of Management Bangalore issued various vacancies for the posts of Academic Associate. The online application for these posts under IIM Bangalore shall start from 22.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 06.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for IIM Bangalore Academic Associate Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 22.04.2021.
  • The Last date to apply online is 06.05.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a Master degree in the relevant field to apply for the post of Academic Associate

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 37 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

IIM Bangalore Academic Associate Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the post of Academic Associate is Rs. 31,000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for IIM Bangalore Academic Associate Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of IIM Bangalore.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Academic Associate.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
