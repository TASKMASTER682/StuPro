Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for graduates have been published by the Indian Army for Short Service Commission vacancies in India. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Short Service Commission, from 25.05.2021, and till the23.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in India and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about application fee

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Short Service Commission in Indian Army, your Degree, B.E/B/Tech  must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 25.05.2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 23.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 20 to 30 years old. 

Indian Army Short Service Commission Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for Join Indian Army SSC (Tech) Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Short Service Commission. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below:

  • First, go to the official website of the Indian Army and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by Indian Army
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
