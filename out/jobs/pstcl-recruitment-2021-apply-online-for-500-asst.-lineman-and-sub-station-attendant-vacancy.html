Total Vacancies:501

Name of the post:

  • Assistant Lineman (ALM), Assistant Sub Station Attendant (ASSA)

Description:

The Punjab State Transmission Corporation Limited issued a total of 500 vacancies for the posts of Assistant Lineman (ALM), Assistant Sub Station Attendant (ASSA). The online application for these posts under PSTCL shall start soon. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 11-06-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for PSTCL Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the General, OBC is Rs. 400/- + Bank Charges
  • The application fee for SC/ST/EWS is Rs. 160/- + Bank Charges
  • The application fee for a Person with a disability is Rs. 200/- + Bank Charges
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • Starting Date for Online Registration of Application: 20-05-2021 from 10:00 am
  • Last Date for Completion of Online Registration/ Step I: 11-06-2021 up to 05:00 pm
  • Last Date for Depositing Fee At SBI (Step II): 15-06-2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have the diploma in 10th/ITI to apply for the post of Assistant Lineman (ALM), Assistant Sub Station Attendant (ASSA)

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 37 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

PSTCL Asst. Lineman & Sub Station Attendant Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Assistant Lineman (ALM), Assistant Sub Station Attendant (ASSA) is Rs. 6400-20,200/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for PSTCL Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of PSTCL.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Assistant Lineman (ALM), Assistant Sub Station Attendant (ASSA).
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
