Description

New Central Government Jobs for 12th pass has been published by National Water Development Agency for the vacancies of JE. If you are searching for a government job in India then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of JE, from 10.05.2021, and till 25.06.2021. If you are interested in all these posts and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for Gen/ OBC candidates is only Rs. 840/-.
  • The application fee for SC/ST/EWS/PWD/Women candidates is only Rs. 500/-.

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for JE vacancies in India, your 12th, Diploma, Degree must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 10.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 25.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 Years to 37 Years old. 

NWDA JE Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 19,900-112400/-.

How to apply for NWDA JE Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the JE. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of NWDA and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by NWDA.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
