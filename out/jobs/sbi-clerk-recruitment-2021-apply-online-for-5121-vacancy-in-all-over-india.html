Description

New Indian Government banking Jobs have been published by the State Bank of India for the posts of Clerk. If you are finding a government job anywhere in India then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for these posts, from 27-04-2021, and till 17-05-2021. If you are interested in all these posts and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for General/ OBC/ EWS candidates is only Rs 750/-
  • No application fee for SC/ ST/ PWD/ XS/ DXS candidates .

Educational qualification to apply for SBI Clerk posts:

  • To apply for a Clerk posts in SBI, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 27-04-2021.
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 20-05-2021.
  • Last Date for Printing Your Application: 01-06-2021
  • Date of Preliminary Examination (Tentative): June 2021
  • Date of Main Examination (Tentative): 31-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • The minimum age should be 20 years.
  • The maximum age should be 28 years.
  • Check out the notification for age relaxation

Detailed vacancies:

SBI Clerk Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Indian Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 20,000 to 22,000/-.

How to apply for SBI Clerk Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process for these posts in India. Fill in your details and submit it

Or follow all the steps given below,

  •  First, go to the official website of SBI and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked BY SBI 
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
