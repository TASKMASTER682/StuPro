Description

New Central Government Jobs for 10th pass,12th pass, and diploma holders have been published by Broadcast Engineering Consultants India Limited for the vacancies of Medical Record Technician. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Medical Record Technician, from 12.05.2021, and till 31.05.2021. If you are interested in all these posts and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • Gen / OBC/Ex-Serviceman/Women Candidates: Rs.750/-
  • SC/ ST/ EWS/PH Candidates: Rs.450/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Medical Record Technician vacancies in Bhopal, your 10th, 12th, Diploma, B.Sc must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 12.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 31.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 30  years old. 

BECIL Medical Record Technician Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 23,550/-.

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Medical Record Technician. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.
