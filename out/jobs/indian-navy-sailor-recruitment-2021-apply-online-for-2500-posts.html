Name of the post:

Description:

Indian Navy issued a total of 2500 vacancies for the posts of sailor. The online application for these posts under the Indian Navy shall start from 26.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 30.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for Indian Navy Sailor Recruitment 2021 :

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for all candidates is Rs. 60+GST
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 26.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 30.04.2021

Educational qualification:

You must have the diploma in 10th/12th standard to apply for the post of sailor

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 45 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Department sailor Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of sailor is INR 14,600/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for Indian Navy Sailor Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of the Indian Navy.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of sailor.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
