Name of the post:

  • Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman & Other vacancies 

Description:

The Border Roads Organization (BRO)  issued a total of 627 vacancies for the posts of Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman & Other vacancies. The online application for the posts of Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman & Other vacancies under Border Roads Organization (BRO) shall start from 18-02-2021. The eligible candidates can apply for the online application within 75 years. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, for Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman & Other vacancies mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The interested candidates after filling the application form, it is required to pay the application fee to complete the form submission process:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the GEN/ EWS/ Ex-servicemen /OBC is INR 50/-
  • No application fee for SC/ST/PwD
  • Payment Mode: through Online in favor of Commandant, GREF Centre, Pune-411 015.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 18-02-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is within 75 days

Age limit criteria:

The interested candidates must have fallen in the age limit criteria provided by the Border Roads Organization (BRO)  is:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply for Sl.No.01, 02, 03, 04, 07 is 25 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply for Sl.No.05, 06 in 25 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman is INR 19,000 to 63000/month

How to apply for BRO Various Vacancy 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.bro.gov.in
  • Register yourself on the portal of Border Roads Organization (BRO).
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Multi Skilled Worker, Store Keeper, Draughtsman & Other vacancies.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
