Total Vacancies:899

Name of the post:

  • Medical Officer

Description:

The Public Health Department, Maharashtra issued a total of 899 vacancies for the posts of Medical Officer. The online application for these posts under Public Health Dept Maharashtra has started. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 20-04-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for Public Health Dept Maharashtra Medical Officer Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the GEN is Rs. 1000/-
  • The application fee for Backward classes is Rs. 500/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Last date to apply online is 20-04-2021

Educational qualification:

  • For Medical Officer (MBBS) Post: Candidates should possess MBBS Degree of a statutory University or any other equivalent qualification
  • For Medical Officer (Specialist post): Candidates should possess a Post Graduate Degree/ Diploma in Statutory University.

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 38 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Medical Officer is INR 17000-75000/MONTH
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

Detailed vacancies:

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that Public Health Dept Maharashtra issued this notification for which posts.

  •  Medical Officer, Group A - 899

How to apply for Public Health Dept Maharashtra Medical Officer Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of Public Health Dept Maharashtra.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Medical Officer.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
