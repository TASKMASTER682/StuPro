Name of the post:

Description:

Central Power Distribution Company of AP Limited  issued a total of 86 vacancies for the posts of Energy Assistants (Junior Linemen Grade-II) . The online application for these posts under APCPDCL shall start from 07-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 03-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for APCPDCL Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for OC/ BC Candidates is 700/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST Candidates is 350/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 07-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 03-05-2021

Educational qualification:

You must have the diploma in SSC/ 10th Class with ITI to apply for the post of Energy Assistants (Junior Linemen Grade-II) 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 18 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 35 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Energy Assistants (Junior Linemen Grade-II)  is: Rs 57,000 per month
