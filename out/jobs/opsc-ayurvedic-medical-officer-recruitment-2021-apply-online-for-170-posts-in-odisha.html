Description

New Odisha Government Jobs have been published by Odisha Public Service Commission for the vacancies of Ayurvedic Medical Officer. If you are finding a government job in Odisha then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Ayurvedic Medical Officer, from 15-05-2021, and till the 15-05 to 25-06-2021 till 11:59 pm. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Odisha and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online.
  • No application for PwD candidates
  • The application fee for other candidates is only Rs. 500/-.

Educational qualification to apply for OPSC Ayurvedic Medical Officer posts in Odisha:

  • To apply for an Ayurvedic Medical Officer posts in Odisha, your qualification must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 15-05-2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is15-05 to 25-06-2021 till 11:59 pm.
  • Last Date to Registration/ Re-registration & Payment of Fee: 18-06-2021 till 11:59 pm

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 32  years old. 

OPSC Ayurvedic Medical Officer Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Odisha Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 9300-34-800/-.

Detailed Vacancies:

  • UR - 100
  • SEBC - Nil
  • SC - 14
  • ST - 56

How to apply for OPSC Ayurvedic Medical Officer Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Ayurvedic Medical Officer. Fill in your details and submit it Or follow all the steps given below,
