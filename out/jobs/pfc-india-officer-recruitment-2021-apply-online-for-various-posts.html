Name of the post:

  • Assistant Officer, Assistant Manager, Deputy Manager, Deputy Officer, Manager, Dy. Manager

Description:

Power Finance Corporation Limited issued a total of 11 vacancies for the posts of Assistant Officer, Assistant Manager, Deputy Manager, Deputy Officer, Manager, Dy. Manager The online application for these posts under PFC shall start from 01.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 23.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com.The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for PFC India Officer Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 01.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 23.04.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have the B.E./B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, CA, CMA, MCA, M.Sc, B.Com, M.Com, MBA, Any Graduate, PG Deg. from a recognized university to apply for the post of Assistant Officer, Assistant Manager, Deputy Manager, Deputy Officer, Manager, Dy. Manager

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 21 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 34 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Assistant Officer, Assistant Manager, Deputy Manager, Deputy Officer, Manager, Dy. Manager is :
  • Assistant Officer – Rs.30000/- to 120000/-
  • Assistant Manager – Rs.60000/- to 180000/-
  • Deputy Manager – Rs.70000/ to 200000/-
  • Deputy Officer – Rs.₹40000 to 1,40,000/-
  • Manager – Rs.80000/- to 2,20,000/-
  • Dy. Manager – Rs.70000/- to 2,00,000/-

How to apply for PFC India Officer Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of PFC
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Assistant Officer, Assistant Manager, Deputy Manager, Deputy Officer, Manager, Dy. Manager
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
