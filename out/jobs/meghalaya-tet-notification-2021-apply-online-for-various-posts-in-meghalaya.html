Description

New Meghalaya Government Jobs have been published by Meghalaya Teacher Eligibility Test for the vacancies of school Teacher. If you are finding a government job in Meghalaya then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for these vacancies in Meghalaya, from 10-06-2021, and till the 10-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Meghalaya and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification to apply for department School Teacher vacancies in Meghalaya:

  • To apply for a School Teacher posts in Meghalaya, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 10-06-2021 at 10:00 am.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 10-07-2021 at 05:00 pm.
  • Date of Exam (Tentative): 28-08-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 37  years old. 

MTET School Teacher Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Meghalaya Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 12000-18000.

How to apply for Meghalaya TET Notification 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process for these vacancies in Meghalaya. Fill in your details and submit it

Or follow all the steps given below,

  •  First, go to the official website of MTET and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by MTET.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
