Description

New New Delhi Government Jobs have been published by The Unique Identification Authority of India for the vacancies of Assistant Account Officer, Accountant, Section Officer, Private Secretary. If you are finding a government job in New Delhi then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for these vacancies in New Delhi, from 24.04.2021., and till 28.05.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in New Delhi and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online as well as offline
  • No information about the application fee

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for an Assistant Account Officer posts in New Delhi, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 24.04.2021.
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 16.07.2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 56 years old. 

UIDAI Assistant Account Officer Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the New Delhi Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 9300- 34800 per month

How to apply for UIDAI Technical Officer Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process for these vacancies in New Delhi. Fill in your details and submit it

  • Or follow all the steps given below,
  •  First, go to the official website of UIDAI  and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked BY UIDAI.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
  • And for offline application, Send your application to the below :
  • “Assistant Director General (HR), Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI), 4th Floor, Bangla Sahib Road, Behind Kali Mandir, Gole Market, New Delhi-110001.”
