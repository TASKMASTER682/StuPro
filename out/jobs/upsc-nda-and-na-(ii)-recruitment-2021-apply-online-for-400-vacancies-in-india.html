Name of the Vacancies:

Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for 10th pass has been published by Union Public Service Commission for National Defense Academy vacancies in India. If you are searching for a government job in India then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of National Defense Academy, from 09-06-2021, and till 29-06-2021 . If you are interested in all these vacancies in India and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for General category candidates is only Rs. 100/-
  • The application fee for SC/ST candidates / Sons of JCOs/NCOs/ORs category candidates is Nil

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a National Defense Academy in UPSC, your 10+2 must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 09-06-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 29-06-2021 

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 40 years old. 

UPSC National Defense Academy Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary

is as per norms

How to apply for UPSC NDA & NA (II) Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the National Defense Academy. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  • First, go to the official website of UPSC and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by UPSC
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
