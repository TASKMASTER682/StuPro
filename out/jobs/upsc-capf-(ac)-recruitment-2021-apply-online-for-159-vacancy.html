Name of the post:

  • Conduct Central Armed Police Forces (Assistant Commandants)

Description:

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) issued a total of 159 vacancies for the posts of conduct Central Armed Police Forces (Assistant Commandants). The online application for these posts under UPSC shall start from 15-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 05-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for UPSC CAPF (AC) Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for General: Rs. 200/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST/ Female: NIL
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 15-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 05-05-2021 

Educational qualification:

  • You must have Graduation to apply for the post of conduct Central Armed Police Forces (Assistant Commandants)

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 20 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 25 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of (Assistant Commandants) is as per norms

How to apply for UPSC CAPF (AC) Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of UPSC
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of conduct Central Armed Police Forces (Assistant Commandants)
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
