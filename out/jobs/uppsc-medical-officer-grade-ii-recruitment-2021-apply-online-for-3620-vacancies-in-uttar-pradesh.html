Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Diploma holders have been published by Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission for Medical Officer Grade-II vacancies in Uttar Pradesh. If you are searching for a government job in Uttar Pradesh then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Medical Officer Grade-II, from 28-05-2021, and till 28-06-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Uttar Pradesh and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for UR/ OBC category candidates is only Rs. 105/- 
  • The application fee for SC/ ST/ Ex Serviceman category candidates is only Rs. 65/- 
  • The application fee for PH Candidates category candidates is only Rs. 25/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Medical Officer Grade-II  in UPPSC, your MBBS, PG Degree/ Diploma must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 28-05-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 28-06-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 40 years old.
