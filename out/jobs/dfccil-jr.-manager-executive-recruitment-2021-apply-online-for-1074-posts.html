Description:

Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited issued a total of 1074 vacancies for the vacancies of Junior Manager, Executive, and Junior Executive. The online application for these vacancies under DFCCIL shall start from 24-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 23-05-2021. Further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these vacancies in India is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for DFCCIL Jr. Manager, Executive Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the Junior Manager (UR/OBC-NCL/EWS) is Rs. 1000/-
  • The application fee for Executive (UR/OBC-NCL/EWS) is Rs. 900/-
  • The application fee for Jr. Executive (UR/OBC-NCL/EWS) is Rs. 700/-
  • No application fee for SC/ST/PwBD/Ex-Servicemen candidates.
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 24-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 23-07-2021 

Age limit criteria:

  • For Jr. Manager: 18 to 27 Years
  • For Executive: 18 to 30 Years
  • For Jr. Executive: 18 to 30 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

DFCCIL Junior Manager, Executive, and Junior Executive Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the vacancies of Junior Manager, Executive, and Junior Executive in India is Rs. 25000-68000/month

How to apply for DFCCIL Jr. Manager, Executive Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of DFCCIL
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Junior Manager, Executive, and Junior Executive.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
