Description

New Central Government Jobs for diploma holders have been published by the Tata Institute of Social Science for the Vacancies of Communication Officer. If you are searching for a government job in Mumbai then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Communication Officer, from 08.05.2021, and till 23.05.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for SC/ST/PWD candidates is only Rs. 250/-.
  • The application fee for the rest of the candidates is only Rs. 1000/-.

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Communication Officer vacancies in Mumbai, your Master’s degree, PG Diploma must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 08.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 23.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 37  years old. 

TISS Communication Officer Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 60,000.

How to apply for TISS Communication Officer Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Communication Officer. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of TISS and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by TISS.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
