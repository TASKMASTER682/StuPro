Description

The latest Karnataka Government Jobs for 12th pass and diploma holders have been published by  Karnataka State Police (KSP) for Constable vacancies in Karnataka. If you are searching for a government job in Karnataka then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Constable, from 25-05-2021, and till 12-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Karnataka and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee

  • The mode of application is Online
  • For GM & OBC (2A, 2B, 3A, 3B): Rs. 400/-
  • For SC/ ST, CAT-01: Rs. 200/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Constable vacancies in Karnataka, your PUC/ HSC, Diploma/ ITI must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  • The starting date to apply for these posts is 25-05-2021 from 10:00 am.
  • The last date to apply in these posts is 12-07-2021 up to 06:00 pm

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 19 to 27  years old. 
  • Maximum Age Limit for Others: 25 & 30 Years

KSP Constable Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Karnataka Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 21000-42000.

Physical Eligibility Details 

Endurance Test:

Constable (Male):

  • 1600 Mtrs Run – 6:30 Minutes
  •  Long Jump/ High Jump – 3.80 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only), 1.20 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only)
  • Short-put (7.26 kgs)- 5.60 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only)  

ET Candidates:

  • For General Candidates: Height – 168 cm, for Tribal Area: 155 cm
  • For General Candidates: Chest – 86 cm, for Tribal Area: 75 cm, – When fully Expansion (Minimum Expansion Must be 5 Cms)
  • Endurance Test

Women & Ex-servicemen:

Constable:

  • 400 Mtrs Run – 2 Minutes
  • Long Jump/ High Jump – 2.50 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only), 0.90 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only)
  • Shortput (4 kgs) – 3.75 Mtrs (In 3 Chances only)

ET Candidates: 

  • For General Candidates: Height – 157 cm, for Tribal Area: 150 cm for Women
  • Chest – 86 Cms – When fully Expansion (Minimum Expansion Must be 5 Cms)-for In & Ex-Serviceman
  • Weight (for Women only) – 45 Kgs

How to apply for KSP Constable Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Constable. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of KSP and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by KSP.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
