Total Vacancies: 34

Name of the post:

  • Technician

Description:

National Aerospace Laboratories (NAL)  issued a total of 34 vacancies for the posts of Technician. The online application for these posts under CSIR-NAL shall start from 20.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 20.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for CSIR-NAL Technician Recruitment 2021 :

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the GEN/OBC is Rs. 100/-
  • No application fee for SC/ST candidates
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 20.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 20.05.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a diploma in Matric/10th to apply for the post of Technician

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 28 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

CSIR-NAL Technician Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Technician is 31000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for CSIR-NAL Technician Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of CSIR-NAL.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Technician.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
