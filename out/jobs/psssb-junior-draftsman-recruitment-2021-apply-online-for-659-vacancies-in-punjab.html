Name of the Vacancies:

  • Junior Draftsman (Civil, Mechanical & Architecture) 

Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by Punjab Subordinate Service Selection Board for Junior Draftsman vacancies in Punjab. If you are searching for a government job in Punjab then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Junior Draftsman, from 08-07-2021, and till 22-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Punjab and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for GEN category candidates is only Rs.1000/-
  • The application fee for SC/BC/ EWS candidates category candidates is only Rs. 250/-
  • The application fee for Ex-Servicemen & Dependent category candidates is only Rs. 200/-
  • The application fee for Physically Handicapped category candidates is only Rs. 500/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Junior Draftsman in PSSSB, your Diploma/ Degree (Civil, Mechanical Engg) must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 08-07-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 22-07-2021 

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 35 years old. 

PSSSB Junior Draftsman Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for PSSSB Junior Draftsman Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Junior Draftsman. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of PSSSB and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by PSSSB.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
