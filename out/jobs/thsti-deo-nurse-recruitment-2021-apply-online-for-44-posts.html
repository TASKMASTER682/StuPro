Description:

Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI) issued a total of 44 vacancies for the posts of DEO, Nurse, SRF. The online application for these posts under THSTI shall start from 13.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 25.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for THSTI DEO, Nurse recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 13.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 25.04.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree to apply for the post of DEO, Nurse, SRF 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 25 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 45 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of DEO, Nurse, SRF is: RS 18,000 - 52,290 Per month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for THSTI DEO, Nurse recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of THSTI
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of DEO, Nurse, SRF
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
