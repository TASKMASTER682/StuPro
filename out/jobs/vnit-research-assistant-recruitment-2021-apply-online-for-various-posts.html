Total Vacancies: Various

Name of the post:

  • Research Assistant

Description:

The Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology issued a total of various vacancies for the posts of Research Assistant. The online application for these posts under VNIT shall start from 01.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 12.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for VNIT Research Assistant Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • No Application Fee
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 01.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 12.04.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree in B.Arch, B.Tech/B.E, M.A to apply for the post of Research Assistant 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 27 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Research Assistant is INR 24000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for VNIT Research Assistant Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of VNIT.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Research Assistant.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
