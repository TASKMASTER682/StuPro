Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Bachelors’ Degree holder has been published by Odisha Police Recruitment Board for Sub Inspector vacancies in Odisha. If you are searching for a government job in Odisha then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Sub Inspector, from 22-06-2021, and till 15-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Odisha and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for SC/ ST Category category candidates is Nil
  • The application fee for All Other Category Category category candidates is only Rs.285/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Sub Inspector  in OPRB, your Bachelors’ Degree must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 22-06-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 15-07-2021 

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 25 years old. 

OPRB Sub Inspector Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for Odisha Police Sub Inspector Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Sub Inspector. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of OPRB and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by OPRB
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
