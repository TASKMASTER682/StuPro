Name of the Vacancies:

Description

The latest Central Government Jobs for Engineers have been published by Gujarat Energy Transmission Corporation Ltd for Vidyut Sahayak vacancies in Gujarat, If you are searching for a government job in Gujarat then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Vidyut Sahayak, from 18-06-2021, and till 04-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Gujarat and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for UR, SEBC & EWS Candidates category candidates is an only Rs. 500/- 
  • The application fee forSC & ST Candidates Candidates category candidates is only Rs. 250/- 

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Vidyut Sahayak in GETCO , your BE/ B.Tech (Electrical, Electricals & Electronics, Civil) must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 18-06-2021
  •  The Last date to apply in these posts is 04-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 27 years old. 

GETCO Vidyut Sahayak Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly

salary is as per norms

How to apply for GETCO Vidyut Sahayak Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Vidyut Sahayak. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of GETCO  and register yourself on the portal.
