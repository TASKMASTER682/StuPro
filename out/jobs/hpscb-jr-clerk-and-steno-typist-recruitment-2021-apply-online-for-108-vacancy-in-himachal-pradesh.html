Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for the 12th Pass have been published by Himachal Pradesh State Co-operative Bank Limited (HPSCB) for Jr Clerk & Steno Typist vacancies in Himachal Pradesh. If you are searching for a government job in Himachal Pradesh then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Jr Clerk & Steno Typist, from 05-06-2021, and till 02-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • For General/ OBC/ Ex Serviceman/ WEF/ PH: Rs. 1000/- 
  • For SC/ST/IRDP/ BPL/ Antodaya/ EWS & Female Candidates: Rs. 800/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Jr Clerk & Steno Typist vacancies in Himachal Pradesh, your 10+2 with Typing speed must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 05-06-2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 02-07-2021
  • Last Date for Editing Application Details: 02-07-2021
  • Last Date for Printing Your Application: 17-07-2021
  • Date for Download Call Letter: Around 07 Days before Exam Date
  • Date for Online Exam: August 2021
  • Date of declaration of result for Online Exam: Around 15 days after the conduct of the Exam

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 45  years old. 

HPSCB Jr Clerk & Steno Typist Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 10300-34800.

How to apply for HPSCB Jr Clerk & Steno Typist Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Jr Clerk & Steno Typist. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of HPSCB and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by HPSCB.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
