Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for 1oth pass has been published by Heavy Engineering Corporation Limited for Trainee vacancies in India. If you are searching for a government job in India then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Trainee, from 25-06-2021, and till 31-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in India and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for Others, OBC (NCL), EWS category candidates is only  Rs. 750/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Trainee in HEC, your 10th class must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 25-06-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 31-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 35 years old. 

HEC Trainee Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for HEC Ltd Trainee Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Trainee. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of HEC and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by HEC
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
