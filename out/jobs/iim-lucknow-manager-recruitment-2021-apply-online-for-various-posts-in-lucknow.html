Description

New Central Government Jobs for the postgraduates have been published by the Indian Institute of Management Lucknow for the vacancies of Program Manager. If you are searching for a government job in Lucknow then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Program Manager, from 08.05.2021, and till 25.05.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for Program Manager vacancies in Lucknow, your Master Degree, Postgraduate must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 08.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 25.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 Years to 37  years old. 

IIM Lucknow Program Manager Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 35000-75000.

How to apply for IIM Lucknow Manager Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Program Manager. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of IIM Lucknow and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by IIM Lucknow.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
