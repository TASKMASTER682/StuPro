Name of the post:

  • Specialist Cadre Officer & Pharmacist regular and contractual basis

Description:

The State Bank of India (SBI)  issued a total of 144 vacancies for the posts of Specialist Cadre Officer & Pharmacist . The online application for these posts under SBI  shall start from 13-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 03-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for SBI Specialist Officer Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the General/ EWC/ OBC is INR 750/-
  • No application fee for SC/ ST/ PwD
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 15-06-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 26-06-2021

Age limit criteria:

  • Age limit criteria is given in detailed vacancies below

SBI Specialist Officer Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Specialist Cadre Officer & Pharmacist is Rs. 50,000-1,46000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

Detailed vacancies:

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that SBI issued this notification for which posts.

 

How to apply for SBI Specialist Officer Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of SBI .
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Specialist Cadre Officer & Pharmacist .
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
