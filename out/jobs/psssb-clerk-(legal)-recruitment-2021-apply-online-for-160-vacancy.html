Description:

Punjab Subordinate Service Selection Board (PSSSB)  issued a total of 160 vacancies for the various vacancies in Punjab. The online application for these vacancies in Punjab under PSSSB shall start from 21-05-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 15-06-2021 upto 5 pm. Further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these vacancies in Punjab is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for PSSSB Clerk (Legal) Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for SC/BC/ EWS candidates: Rs. 250/-
  • The application fee for GEN: Rs.1000/-
  • The application fee for Ex-Servicemen & Dependent: Rs. 200/-
  • The application fee for Physical Handicapped: Rs. 500/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 21-05-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 15-06-2021 upto 5 pm
  • Last Date to Fee Payment: 18-06-2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have Any Degree with Punjabi up to matriculation to apply for the post of PSSSB

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 37 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

PSSSB Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the various posts released by PSSSB is as per norms
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for PSSSB Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of PSSSB
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the block-level extension officer and others
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
