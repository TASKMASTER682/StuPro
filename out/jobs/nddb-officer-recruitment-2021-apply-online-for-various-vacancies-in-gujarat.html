Latest Central Government Jobs for Engineers has been published by National Dairy Development Board for Officer vacancies in Gujarat. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Gujarat and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about application fee

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for an Officer in NDDB, your B.Tech/B.E, M.Sc, M.E/M.Tech, MCA must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is the 21.05.2021

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Officer. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.
