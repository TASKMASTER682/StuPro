Total Vacancies:135

Name of the post:

  • Master Cadre (Hindi, English, Maths, Science) 

Description:

The School Education Dept, Punjab issued a total of 135 vacancies for the posts of Master Cadre (Hindi, English, Maths, Science). The online application for these posts under School Education Dept, Punjab shall start from 01-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 21-04-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for School Education Dept Punjab Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the GEN & Other Category is Rs. 1000/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST Category is Rs. 500/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 01-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 21-04-2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree in B.Ed, Functional English, English Literature, B.A. Honors (English), B.A. (Honours) English, B.Sc to apply for the post of Master Cadre (Hindi, English, Maths, Science) 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 47 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Master Cadre (Hindi, English, Maths, Science)  is INR 10,300 to 1,23,600/MONTH
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for School Education Dept Punjab Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of School Education Dept, Punjab.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Master Cadre (Hindi, English, Maths, Science).
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
