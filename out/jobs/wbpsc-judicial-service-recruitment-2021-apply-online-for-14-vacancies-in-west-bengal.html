Description

The Latest Central Government Jobs for Degree of law holder have been published by West Bengal Public Service Commission for Judicial Service vacancies in West Bengal. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Judicial Service, from 14-07-2021, and till 05-08-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in West Bengal and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about the application fee

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Judicial Service in WBPSC, your Degree (Law) must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 14-07-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 05-08-2021

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Judicial Service. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.
