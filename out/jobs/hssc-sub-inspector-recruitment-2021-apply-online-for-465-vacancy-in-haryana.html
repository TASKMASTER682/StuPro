Description

The latest Haryana Government Jobs for the 10th pass has been published by Haryana Staff Selection Commission for Sub-Inspector of Haryana vacancies in Haryana. If you are searching for a government job in Haryana then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Ex-Serviceman of Haryana, from 15-06-2021, and till 09-07-2021 by 11:59 PM. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Haryana and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee
