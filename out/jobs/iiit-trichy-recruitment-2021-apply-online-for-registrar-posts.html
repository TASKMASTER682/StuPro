Description

New Trichy Government Jobs have been published by the Indian Institute of Information Trichy for the vacancies of Registrar & Assistant Registrar. If you are finding a government job in Trichy then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for these vacancies in Trichy, from 04.05.2021, and till 04.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Trichy and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about application fee

Educational qualification to apply for IIIT Registrar & Assistant Registrar vacancies in Trichy:

  • To apply for a Registrar & Assistant Registrar posts in Trichy, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is the 04.05.2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 04.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • The minimum age should be 27 years.
  • The maximum age should be 40 years.

IIIT Registrar & Assistant Registrar Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Trichy Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is
  • Registrar – Rs.37400/ to Rs.67000/-
  • Assistant Registrar – Rs.15600/ to Rs.39100/-

How to apply for IIIT Trichy Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process for these vacancies in Trichy. Fill in your details and submit it

  • Or follow all the steps given below,
  •  First, go to the official website of IIIT  and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked BY IIIT 
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
