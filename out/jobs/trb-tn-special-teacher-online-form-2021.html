Name of the posts:

  • Special Teacher 

Description:

The Teachers Recruitment Board issued a total of 1598 for the posts of Special Teacher The online application for the posts of Special Teacher under TRB TN shall start from 31-03-2021. The eligible candidates must submit the online application before 25-04-2021. Check the complete details below so that all the doubts about the filling of the online application get cleared. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com.The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The mode of application is online and after filling in the required details you have to provide the billing details for the application fee.

  • The application fee for the SC/ SCA/ ST & PWD is 250/-
  • The application fee for others is 500/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply for this online application form is 31-03-2021
  • The Last date to apply for this online application form is 25-04-2021.

Educational qualification required:

To apply for the posts issued by the TRB TN  you must have fallen in the educational criteria given below:

  • You must have a diploma in Physical Education to apply for the post of Special Teacher 

Age limit criteria:

To apply for this job you must have fallen in the age limit criteria provided by the TRB TN  :

  • The minimum age limit to apply is: 18 years
  • The maximum age limit to apply is: 40 years
  • For age relaxation criteria you can refer to the notification given below in The ProGrad jobs.\

Pay Scale:

The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of special Teacher  is:

  • As per norms
  • Check the detailed notification also if we missed something

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that the TRB TN issued this notification for which posts.

How to apply for TRB TN Special Teacher Online Form 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of the Teachers Recruitment Board.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Special Teacher 
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
