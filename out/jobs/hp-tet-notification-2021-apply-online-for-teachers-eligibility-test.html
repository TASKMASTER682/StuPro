Description

Latest recruitment notification for the 12th pass has been published by the HP Board of School Education(HP TET) for various vacancies in HP. If you are searching for a government job in HP then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for this notification, from 24-05-2021, and till 13-06-2021 (till 11:59 PM). If you are interested in all these vacancies in HP and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • For General & their Subcategories: Rs.800/-
  • For OBC/ ST/ SC/ PHH: Rs. 500/- 
  • Service Charges & Service Tax if applicable will be charged extra by the Bank.

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 24-05-2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 13-06-2021 (till 11:59 PM)
  • Last Date to Apply Online with Late Fee Rs. 300/-: 14 to 18-06-2021 (till 11:59 PM)
  • Period for online corrections in Particulars: 19-06-2021 to 21-06-2021 (till 11:59 PM)
  •  Download admit card from Board Website: 4 days before the exam.
  • Date for JBT & Sanskrit TET: 04-07-2021
  • Date for TGT (Non-Medical) & Language Teacher TET: 10-07-2021
  • Date for TGT (Arts & Medical) TET: 11-07-2021
  • Date for Punjabi & Urdu TET: 18-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • There is no upper age limit to apply for the TET. The age criteria are to be considered by the appointing authority at the time of appointment as per R&P Rules.

How to apply for HP TET Notification 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the HP TET. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
