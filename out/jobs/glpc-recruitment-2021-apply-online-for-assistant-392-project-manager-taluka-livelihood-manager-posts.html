Name of the post:

Description:

Gujarat Livelihood Promotion Company (GLPC)  issued a total of 392 vacancies for the posts of Assistant Project Manager, Taluka Livelihood Manager . The online application for these posts under GLPC shall start from 08-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 22-04-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for GLPC Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 08-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 22-04-2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree or post graduation to apply for the post of Assistant Project Manager, Taluka Livelihood Manager 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 30 Years

For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

How to apply for GLPC Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of GLPC
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Assistant Project Manager, Taluka Livelihood Manager
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
