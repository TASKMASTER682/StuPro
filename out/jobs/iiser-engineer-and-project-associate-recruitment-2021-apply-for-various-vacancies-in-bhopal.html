Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Engineers has been published by the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research for Engineer and Project Associate vacancies in Bhopal and Berhampur. If you are searching for a government job in Bhopal and Berhampur then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Engineer and Project Associate, from 23.05.2021, and till 21.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Bhopal and Berhampur and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about application fee

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for an Engineer and Project Associate vacancies in Bhopal and Berhampur, your B.E/ B.Tech, M.Phil/Ph.D. must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 23.05.2021.
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 21.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 27 to 50 years old. 

IISER Engineer and Project Associate Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 25000 – 31000

How to apply for IISER Engineer and Project Associate Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly download the application form there and easily start the application process of the Engineer and Project Associate. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of IISER and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by IISER
  • Print out the application form
  • Fill up the details asked in the application form by blue or black pen
  • Now send this application form to the address mentioned in the official notification
