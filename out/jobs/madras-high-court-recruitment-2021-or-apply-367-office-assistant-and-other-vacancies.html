Name of the post:

  • Chobdar, Office Assistant, Cook, Waterman, Room Boy, Watchman, Book Restorer & Library Attendant posts

Description:

The High Court of Madras issued a total of 367 vacancies for the posts of office assistant, cook, and other posts. The online application for the posts of office assistant, cook, and other posts under the High Court of Madras shall start from 14.03.2021. The eligible candidates can apply for the online application before 28.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, for office assistant, cook and other posts mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The interested candidates after filling the application form, it is required to pay the application fee to complete the form submission process:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • No application fee for the ST/ SC(A) & ST/ Different Abled person/ Destitute Widows.
  • The application fee for others is INR 500/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 14.03.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 28.04.2021

Educational qualification required:

Candidates who are interested in office assistant, cook, and other posts should have the following qualification from a recognized University/boards with marks :

  • You must have the passed 8th standard to apply for the post of office assistant, cook, and other posts

Age limit criteria:

The interested candidates must have fallen in the age limit criteria provided by the High Court of Madras is:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 30 years
  • For SC(A)/ ST/ MBC&DC/ BC/ BCM/ Destitute Widow of all castes is 18 to 35 years 
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of office assistant, cook, and other posts is INR 15,700 to 50,000/MONTH
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for Madras High Court Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of the High Court of Madras.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of office assistant, cook, and other posts.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
