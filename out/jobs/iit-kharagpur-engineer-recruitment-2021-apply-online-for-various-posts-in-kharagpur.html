Description

New Central Government Jobs for Engineers has been published by the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur for the vacancies of Senior Project Associate. If you are searching for a government job in Kharagpur then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Senior Project Associate from 20.05.2021, and till 11.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Kharagpur and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • The application fee is Rs. 50

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Senior System Project Associate vacancies in Kharagpur, your B.E/ B.Tech, M.Sc, MCA must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these vacancies is 20.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these vacancies is 24.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 37  years old. 

IIT Kharagpur Senior Project Associate Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 45000.

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Senior System Engineer. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.
