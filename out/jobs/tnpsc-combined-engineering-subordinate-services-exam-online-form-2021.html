Name of the posts:

Description:

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) issued a total of 537 vacancies for the posts of Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Technical Assistant, Junior Engineer. The online application for the posts of Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Technical Assistant, Junior Engineer under TNPSC has started. The eligible candidates must submit the online application before 04-04-2021. Check the complete details below so that all the doubts about the filling of the online application get cleared. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The mode of application is online and after filling in the required details you have to provide the billing details for the application fee.

  • The application fee is Rs. 250/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST/ PWD/ ESM & Other is NIL
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply for this online application form is 05-03-2021
  • The Last date to apply for this online application form is 04-04-2021
  • The date for Written Exam (Paper I & II): 06-06-2021
  • The date for Publication of Written Examination Result: Jul, 2021
  • The date for Certificate upload for Certificate Verification: Aug, 2021
  • The date for Certificate Verification: Sep, 2021
  • The date for Counselling: Oct, 2021

Educational qualification required:

To apply for the posts issued by the TNPSC you must have fallen in the educational criteria given below:

  • You must have the diploma/degree in civil engineering to apply for the post of Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Technical Assistant, Junior Engineer

Age limit criteria:

To apply for this job you must have fallen in the age limit criteria provided by the TNPSC  :

  • The minimum age limit to apply is:21 years
  • The maximum age limit for SC/SC(A)/ST for Post Code 3115 to apply is: 33 years
  • The maximum age limit for MBC/DC / BC/BCM for Post Code 3115 to apply is: 32 years
  • The maximum age limit for SC, SC(A), ST, MBs/DCs, BC, BCM & Destitute Widows of all castes to apply is: 35 years
  • The maximum age limit for others to apply is: 30 years
  • For age relaxation criteria you can refer to the notification given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Draughting Officer, Junior Technical Assistant, Junior Engineer is Rs. 35,900-113500
  • Check the detailed notification also if we missed something

How to apply for TNPSC Combined Engineering Subordinate Services Exam Online Form 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC).
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the combined Engineering Subordinate Services Exam.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
