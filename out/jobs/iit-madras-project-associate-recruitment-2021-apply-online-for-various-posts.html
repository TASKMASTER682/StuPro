Description:

Indian Institute of Technology Madras issued a total of 03 vacancies for the posts of Project Associate. The online application for these posts under IIT Madras shall start from 20.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 03.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for IIT Madras Project Associate Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • No information provided
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 20.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 03.05.2021

IIT Madras Project Associate Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree in B.E/B.Tech/Ph.D  to apply for the post of  Project Associate 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 37 Years

For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

IIT Madras  Project Associate Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the post of Project Associate is INR 21,000-50,000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for IIT Madras Project Associate Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of IIT Madras.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Project Associate.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
