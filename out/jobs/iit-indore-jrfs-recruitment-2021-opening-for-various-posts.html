Total Vacancies: Various

Name of the post:

  • Junior Research Fellow

Description:

Indian Institute of Technology issued a total of various vacancies for the posts of Junior Research Fellow. The online application for these posts under IIT Indore shall start from 20.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 20.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Application fee:

The application fee for IIT Indore JRFs Recruitment 2021 :

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 20.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 20.05.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have the Bachelor degree, Master Degree to apply for the post of JRFs

JRFs Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 37 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

IIT Indore JRFs Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of JRFs is INR 31000/MONTH
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for IIT Indore JRFs Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of IIT Indore.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of JRFs.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
