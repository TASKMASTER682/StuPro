Description

New Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India for the Vacancies of Executive in India. If you are searching for a government job in New Delhi then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the Vacancies of Executive, from 16.04.2021, and till 07.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for Executive vacancies in New Delhi, your Bachelor / Master Degree must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 16.04.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 07.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 37  years old. 

FSSAI Executive Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 35,400-1,12000/-.

How to apply for FSSAI Executive Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Executive. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of FSSAI and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by FSSAI.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
