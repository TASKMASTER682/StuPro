Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Diploma holders has been published by Oil India Ltd for Junior Assistant vacancies in India. If you are interested in all these vacancies in India and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for category General/ OBC candidates is only Rs.200/-
  • The application fee for category SC/ ST/ EWS/ PWD/ Ex-Servicemen Candidates candidates is only Nil

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Junior Assistant in Oil India Ltd, your 10+2, Diploma must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 15-08-2021 at 11:59 pm
