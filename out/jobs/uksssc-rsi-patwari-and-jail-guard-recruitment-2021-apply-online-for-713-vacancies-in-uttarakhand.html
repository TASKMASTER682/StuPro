Name of the Vacancies:

Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by Uttarakhand Subordinate Service Selection Commission for Revenue Sub Inspector vacancies in Uttarakhand. If you are searching for a government job in Uttarakhand then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Revenue Sub Inspector, from 22-06-2021, and till 05-08-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Uttarakhand and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for General/ Uttarakhand OBC category candidates is only Rs. 300/-
  • The application fee for Uttarakhand SC/ ST & PwD category candidates is only Rs. 150/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Revenue Sub Inspector in UKSSSC, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 22-06-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 05-08-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 35 years old. 

UKSSSC Revenue Sub Inspector Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for UKSSSC RSI, Patwari & Jail Guard Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Revenue Sub Inspector. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of UKSSSC and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by UKSSSC
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
