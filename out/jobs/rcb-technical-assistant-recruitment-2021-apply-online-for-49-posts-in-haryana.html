Description

New Central Government Jobs for Graduates and Engineers have been published by Regional Centre for Biotechnology for the vacancies of Technical Assistant. If you are searching for a government job in Haryana or Delhi then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Technical Assistant, from 07.05.2021, and till 31.05.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for various technical Assistant vacancies in Haryana/Delhi, your Graduate, Post-Graduate, B.E/B.Tech, M.E/M.Tech  must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 07.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 31.05.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 18 to 50  years old. 

RCB Technical Assistant Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 2,04,416.

How to apply for RCB Technical Assistant Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Technical Assistant. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of RCB and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by RCB.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
