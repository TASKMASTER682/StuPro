Name of the post:

Description:

Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan issued various vacancies for the posts of Teacher. The online application for these posts under Kerala TET shall start from 28-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 06-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Application fee:

The application fee for Kerala TET Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the General/ OBC is Rs. 500/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST is Rs. 250/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 28-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 06-05-2021

KTET Educational qualification:

For Paper/ Category 1 – Lower Primary Classes (Class 1-5):

  • 12th/ 10+2 (or it’s equivalent) with a minimum of 45% marks
  • Higher Secondary/ Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks and 2-year Diploma in Education (Special Education).
  • 12th/ 10+2 (or it’s equivalent) with at least 45% marks along with a qualifying 2-year diploma in elementary education
  • 12th/ 10+2 (or it’s equivalent) with at least 50% marks and a degree in 4 years Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed.)

For Paper/ Category II – Upper Primary Classes (Class 6-8)

  • BA/ B.Sc./ B.Com. Graduation degree and 2-year diploma Elementary Education/ Trained Teachers’ Certificate (TTC)
  • Qualified 12th or Senior Secondary (or its equivalent) with at least 50% marks along with appearing and qualifying four year Bachelor in Elementary Education (B.El.Ed)
  • Qualified graduation in B.A/ B.Sc/ B.Com with a minimum of 45% marks and possess one year Bachelor in Education (B.Ed.)
  • Higher Secondary/ Senior Secondary (or it’s equivalent) with at least 50% marks and four-year B.A/B.Sc.Ed or B.A. Ed or B.Sc.Ed.

Paper/ Category III – High School Classes (Class 9-12)

  • Graduation with BA/ BSc/ B.Com degree securing at least 45% marks along with a B.Ed.
  • Degree in the subject concerned with MSc.Ed. Degree.

Paper/ Category IV – High School Classes (Class 9-12)

  • Candidates, possessing a Certificate/ Diploma/ Degree in teaching in any subject, and are approved by Universities/ NCTE/ Board of Examinations/ Government of Kerala, are eligible to apply.
  • Those candidates who have acquired qualifications for the posts of Language Teachers of Arabic, Hindi, Sanskrit & Urdu (up to Upper Primary level), Specialist teachers & Physical Education teachers as mentioned in Chapter XXXI of Kerala Education Act And Rules can apply for Kerala TET 2021 Paper IV.

For more Qualification Details Refer to the notification.

How to apply for Kerala TET Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of Kerala TET.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Teacher.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
