Description:

The Karnataka High Court issued a total of 94 vacancies for the posts of Civil Judge (Karnataka Judicial Service). The online application for these posts under Karnataka High Court has started. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 27-04-2021. Mode of application & application fee:

The interested candidates after filling the application form, it is required to pay the application fee to complete the form submission process:

Mode of application: Online

  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Last date to apply online is 27-04-2021 
  • Last Date for Payment of Fee through online: 30-04-2021
  • Last Date for payment of fee through Challan: 30-04-2021 (closing of Banks working hours)

Educational qualification required:

Candidates who are interested to apply should have the following qualification from a recognized University/boards with valid marks :

  • You must have a degree in law and must be enrolled as an advocate to apply for the post of Civil Judge (Karnataka Judicial Service)

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply or SC/ ST is: 35 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply or others is 38 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

How to apply for Karnataka High Court Civil Judge Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of Karnataka High Court.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Civil Judge (Karnataka Judicial Service).
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
