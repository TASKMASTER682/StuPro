Name of the post:

  • Station Fire Officer, Fireman, Driver, Clerk

Description:

The Municipal Corporation Chandigarh issued a total of 172 vacancies for the posts of Station Fire Officer, Fireman, Driver, Clerk. The online application for these posts under Municipal Corporation Chandigarh has started. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 03-05-2021 up to 5 pm. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for Municipal Corporation Chandigarh Various Vacancy Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the SC, PwD & Ex-Serviceman is Rs. 500/-
  • The application fee for General/OBC/ESM(Lineal Descendants)/ EWS is Rs. 1000/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 09-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 03-05-2021 up to 5 pm

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 37 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Municipal Corporation Chandigarh various vacancy Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Station Fire Officer, Fireman, Driver, Clerk is Rs. 8000-24000/month

How to apply for Municipal Corporation Chandigarh Various Vacancy Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of Municipal Corporation Chandigarh.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Municipal Corporation Chandigarh.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
