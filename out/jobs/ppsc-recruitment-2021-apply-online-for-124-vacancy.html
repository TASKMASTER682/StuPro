Description:

The Punjab Public Service Commission (PPSC)  issued a total of 124 vacancies for the posts of Sub Divisional Engineer, Sub Divisional Officer, Assistant Corporation Engineer, Assistant Municipal Engineer, Assistant Trust Engineer. The online application for the posts of Sub Divisional Engineer, Sub Divisional Officer, Assistant Corporation Engineer, Assistant Municipal Engineer, Assistant Trust Engineer under Punjab Public Service Commission (PPSC) has started. The eligible candidates can apply for the online application before 09-04-2021. Mode of application & application fee:

The interested candidates after filling the application form, it is required to pay the application fee to complete the form submission process:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the SC/ ST of All States & BC of Punjab is INR 1125/-
  • The application fee for Ex-Servicemen of Punjab is INR 500/-
  • The application fee for PWD of Punjab is INR 1750/-
  • For all other categories, the application is INR 3000/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online has started
  • The Last date to apply online is 09-04-2021

Educational qualification required:

Candidates who are interested in Sub Divisional Engineer, Sub Divisional Officer, Assistant Corporation Engineer, Assistant Municipal Engineer, Assistant Trust Engineer should have the following qualification from a recognized University/boards :

  • For S. No 09: B.Tech (Civil)
  • For S.No 10 to 13: Degree (Civil)
  •  For S.No 15: B.E/ B.Tech (Electrical)
  •  For S.No 16: B.E/ B.Tech (Mechanical)
  • For S.No 17, 18: B.E/ B.Tech (Civil)
