Name of the Vacancies:

Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Engineers have been published by Uttar Pradesh Rajya Vidyut Utpadan Nigam Limited for Jr Engineer (Trainee) vacancies in Uttar Pradesh. If you are searching for a government job in Uttar Pradesh then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Jr Engineer (Trainee) from 02-07-2021, and till 11-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Uttar Pradesh and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for UR/ OBC/ EWS category candidates is only 1000/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST (Residents of UP) category candidates is only Rs.700/-
  • The application fee for PwD Category (Residents of UP) category candidates is only Rs. 10/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for a Computer Engineering. in UPRVUNL, your Electrical/ Mechanical/ Electronics/ Control & Instrumentation/ Computer Engineering must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is the 02-07-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 11-07-2021 

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 40 years old. 

UPRVUNL Jr Engineer (Trainee) Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for UPRVUNL Jr Engineer Trainee Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of Jr Engineer (Trainee). Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of UPRVUNL and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by UPRVUNL
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
