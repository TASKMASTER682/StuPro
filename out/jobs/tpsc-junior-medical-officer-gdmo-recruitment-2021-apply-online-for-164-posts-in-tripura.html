Name of the Vacancies:

Description

New Tripura Government Jobs for MBBS persued have been published by Tripura Public Service Commission (TPSC) for the vacancies of Junior Medical Officer- General Duty Medical Officer, Gr-IV in Tripura. If you are searching for a government job in Tripura then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Junior Medical Officer/ GDMO, from 10-05-2021, and till17-05-2021 up to 4 pm. If you are interested in all these posts and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for General & Other States of Tripura candidates is only Rs.300/-.
  • The application fee for ST/ SC/ BPL/ PH candidates is only Rs.250/-.

Educational qualification :

  • To apply for a Junior Medical Officer/ GDMO vacancies in Tripura, your MBBS must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 10-05-2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 20-05-2021 up to 4 pm
  • Date for Interview: from 25-05-2021
  • Availability of Eligible Candidates List for Interview: 20-05-2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 22 to 40  years old. 

TPSC Junior Medical Officer/ GDMO Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Tripura Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 15,600-39,100.

How to apply for TPSC Junior Medical Officer/ GDMO Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Junior Medical Officer/ GDMO. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of TPSC
  • and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by TPSC.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
