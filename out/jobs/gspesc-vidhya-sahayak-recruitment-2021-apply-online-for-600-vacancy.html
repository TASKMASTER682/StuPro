Total Vacancies:600

Name of the post:

  • Vidhya Sahayak 

Description:

Gujarat State Primary Education Selection Commission issued a total of 600 vacancies for the posts of Vidhya Sahayak 

The online application for these posts under GSPESC shall start from 06-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 19-04-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for GSPESC Vidhya Sahayak Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the SC/ST is 100/-
  • The application fee for General / UR is 200/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 06-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 19-04-2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a degree in PTC/ D.El.Ed or B.El.Ed, B.A.Ed/ B.Com.Ed/ B.R.S.Ed/ B.S.Sc.Ed), TET I/ II to apply for the post of Vidhya Sahayak

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 18 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 38 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

GSPESC Vidhya Sahayak PayScale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the post of Vidhya Sahayak is: Rs19,950per month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

Detailed vacancies:

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that GSPESC issued this notification for which posts.

How to apply for GSPESC Vidhya Sahayak Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of GSPESC
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Vidhya Sahayak
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
