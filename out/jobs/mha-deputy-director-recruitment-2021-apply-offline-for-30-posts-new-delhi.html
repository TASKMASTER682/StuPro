Description

New Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by the Ministry of Home Affairs for the vacancies of Deputy Director. If you are searching for a government job in New Delhi then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Deputy Director, from 17.05.2021, and till 09.07.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in New Delhi and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee

  • Mode of application is Offline

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for MHA vacancies in New Delhi, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 17.05.2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 09.07.2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 65  years old. 

MHA Deputy Director Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ 67,700 – 208,700/-.

Address:

  • Custodian of Enemy Property for India (CEPI), Delhi Head Office, ‘East’ Wing, 1st floor, Shivaji Stadium Annexe, Connaught Place, New Delhi-10001.

How to apply for MHA Deputy Director Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly download the application form there. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  • First, go to the official website of MHA  and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by MHA.
  • Print out the application form
  • Fill up the details asked in the application form by blue or black pen
  • Now send this application form to the address mentioned in the official notification
