Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board for Asst Teacher vacancies in Delhi. If you are searching for a government job in Delhi then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Asst Teacher, from the 25-05-2021, and till the 10-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Delhi and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for Women, SC, ST, PWD & Ex-Serviceman category candidates is Nil
  • The application fee for all other category candidates is only Rs100/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for an Asst Teacher in DSSSB, your Graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 25-05-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 10-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 40 years old. 

DSSSB Asst Teacher Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for DSSSB TGT, Asst Teacher & Other Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Asst Teacher. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.
