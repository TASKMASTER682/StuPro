Description

New Central Government Jobs have been published by the National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh for the vacancies of Associate Professor. If you are finding a government job in Andhra Pradesh then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for these vacancies in Andhra Pradesh, from 20.05.2021, and till 16.06.2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Andhra Pradesh and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.


Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • No information about the application fee

Educational qualification to apply for NIT Associate Professor vacancies in Andhra Pradesh:

  • To apply for an Associate Professor vacancy in Andhra Pradesh, your B.E/B. Tech, M.E/M.Tech, Ph.D. must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply for these posts is 20.05.2021.
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 16.06.2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 40 years old. 

NIT Associate Professor Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹73,100 – 1,01,500 per month

How to apply for NIT Assistant Professor Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process for these vacancies in Andhra Pradesh. Fill in your details and submit it

  • Or follow all the steps given below,
  •  First, go to the official website of NIT and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked BY NIT.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
