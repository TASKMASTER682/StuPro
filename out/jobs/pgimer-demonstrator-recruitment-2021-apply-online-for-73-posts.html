Total Vacancies:73

Name of the post:

  • Junior & Senior Demonstrator & Others

Description:

Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education & Research issued a total of 73 vacancies for the posts of Junior Demonstrator & Others. The online application for these posts under PGIMER shall start from 19.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 10.05.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for PGIMER Demonstrator Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the Gen/OBC  is Rs. 1500/-
  • The application fee for SC/ST  is Rs. 800/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 19.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 10.05.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have completed a Master Degree, MBBS, Ph.D. to apply for the post of Junior Demonstrator & Others

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 45 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

PGIMER Demonstrator Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Junior Demonstrator & Others is Rs. 35,400-67,700/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for PGIMER Demonstrator Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of PGIMER.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Junior Demonstrator & Others.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
