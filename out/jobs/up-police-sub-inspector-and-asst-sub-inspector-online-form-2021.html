Name of the post:

Description:

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment Board (UPPRPB)  issued a total of 1277 vacancies for the vacancies of Sub Inspector (Confidential), Asst Sub-Inspector of Police (Accounts & Clerk). The online application for these vacancies in Uttar Pradesh under UPPRPB  shall start from 01-05-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 15-06-2021. Further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these vacancies in Uttar Pradesh is mentioned below. Mode of application & application fee:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for all candidates is INR 400/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 01-05-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 15-06-2021 

Educational qualification required:

Candidates who are interested to apply should have the following qualification from a recognized University/boards with valid marks :

For SI (Confidential & Clerk) Posts:

  •  Candidates should possess Bachelor's Degree in Any Stream from Any Recognized University in India & Typing Knowledge.

For ASI (Accounts) Posts:

  •  Candidates should possess Bachelor's Degree (Commerce) from Any Recognized University in India & Typing Knowledge. 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 28 Years
