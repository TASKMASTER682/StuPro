Total Vacancies: Various

Name of the post:

  • Laboratory Assistant

Description:

Indian Institute of Technology Jammu issued various vacancies for the posts of Laboratory Assistant. The online application for these posts under IIT Jammu shall start from 20.04.2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 30.04.2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for IIT Jammu Assistant Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 20.04.2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 30.04.2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have completed Bachelor’s Degree, Master’s degree  to apply for the post of Laboratory Assistant

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 21 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 35 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

IIT Jammu Laboratory Assistant Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the post of Laboratory Assistant is Rs. 30,000-35,000/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

How to apply for IIT Jammu Assistant Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of IIT Jammu.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Laboratory Assistant.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
