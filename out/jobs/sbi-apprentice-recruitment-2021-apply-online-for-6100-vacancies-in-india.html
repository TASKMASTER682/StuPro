Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by the State Bank of India for Apprentice vacancies in India. If you are searching for a government job in India then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Apprentice, from 06-07-2021, and till 26-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in India and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee:

  • The mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for all category candidates is only Rs 300/-

Educational qualification:

  • To apply for an Apprentice in SBI, your graduation must be completed from a recognized University

Important dates:

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 06-07-2021
  •  The last date to apply in these posts is 26-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria:

  • For General Candidate, you should be between 21 to 37 years old. 

SBI Apprentice Pay Scale:

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is as per norms

How to apply for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the Apprentice. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of SBI and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by SBI
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
