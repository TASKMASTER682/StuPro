Total Vacancies:2621

Name of the post:

  • Group C Staff Nurse (Male & Female) 

Description:

Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education (UBTER)  issued a total of 2621 vacancies for the posts of Staff Nurse. The online application for these posts under UBTER shall start from 18-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 02-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com

The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for UBTER Staff Nurse Recruitment 2021 :

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for UR (General)/ OBC: Rs.800/-
  • The application fee for SC/ST/ PWD/ EWS: Rs. 400/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 18-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 02-05-2021
  • Last Date for Printing filled-up form: 03-05-2021
  • Dates for Written Exam: 3rd Week of May 2021

Educational qualification:

  • You must have a Degree in B.Sc (Hons) or B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing or General Nursing and Midwifery/ Psychiatry from Uttarakhand/ Indian Nursing & Nursing Council to apply for the post of Staff Nurse 

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is: 21 years
  • The maximum age required to apply is: 37 years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Staff Nurse is: as per norms
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

Detailed vacancies:

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that UBTER issued this notification for which posts.

  •   Staff Nurse (Group C) - 2621

How to apply for UBTER Staff Nurse Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of UBTER.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Staff Nurse
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
