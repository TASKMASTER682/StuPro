Total Vacancies:584

Name of the post:

  • Fisheries Officer, Ophthalmic Assistant, Fisheries Development Officer

Description:

The Bihar Technical Service Commission issued a total of 584 vacancies for the posts of Fisheries Officer, Ophthalmic Assistant, Fisheries Development Officer. The online application for these posts under BTSC shall start from 06-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 05-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for BTSC Bihar Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the General/ OBC is Rs. 200/-
  • The application fee for SC/ ST/ EBC/ Women in Bihar is Rs.50/-
  • For more details, you can refer to the notification. The link is given at the bottom.

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 06-04-2021
  • The Last date to apply online is 05-05-2021

Age limit criteria:

  • Minimum Age Limit for Advt No. 01/2021: 18 Years
  • Minimum Age Limit for Advt No. 02 & 03/2021: 21 Years
  •  Maximum Age Limit for General: 37 Years
  • Maximum Age Limit for OBC/ EBC/ General (Female): 40 Years
  • Maximum Age Limit for SC/ ST: 42 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

BTSC Fisheries Officer, Physiotherapist Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per 7th pay commission for the post of Fisheries Officer, Ophthalmic Assistant, Fisheries Development Officers is Rs. 9400-34,500/month

How to apply for BTSC Bihar Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of BTSC.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of Fisheries Officer, Ophthalmic Assistant, Fisheries Development Officer.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
