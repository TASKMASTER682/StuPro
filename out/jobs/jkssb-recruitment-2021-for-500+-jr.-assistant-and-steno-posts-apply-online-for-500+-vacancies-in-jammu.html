Description

Latest Central Government Jobs for Graduates have been published by J&K Services Selection Board (JKSSB) for Jr Assistant, Steno vacancies in Jammu and Kashmir. If you are searching for a government job in Jammu and Kashmir then it is an opportunity for you. You can fill the online application for the vacancies of Jr Assistant, Steno, from 04-06-2021, and till 31-07-2021. If you are interested in all these vacancies in Jammu and Kashmir and if eligible, please check out the details given below, which have been given age limit, educational qualification pay scale, etc.

Application fee

  • Mode of application is Online
  • The application fee for General candidates is only Rs.350/-.

Educational qualification:

  • Junior Assistant, General Administration Department- Graduation from any recognized University with the knowledge of type writing having not less than 35 words speed per minute. Six months Certificate Course in Computer Applications from a recognized Institute.
  • Jr Steno, Department of Law , Justice and Parliamentary Affairs - Graduate from any recognized University having minimum speed of 65 and 35 words per minute in shorthand and type writing respectively
  • For educational qualification of other posts information, check detailed notification link above.

Important dates

  •  The starting date to apply in these posts is 04-06-2021.
  •  Last date to apply in these posts is 31-07-2021

Age Limit Criteria

  • For OM: 40 Years
  • For SC/ ST/ RBA/ ALC/ IB/ EWS/ PSP/ Social Caste: 43 Years
  • For Physically Challenged Person: 42 Years
  • For Ex-Servicemen: 48 Years
  • For Government Service/Contractual Employment: 40 Years

JKSSB Jr Assistant, Steno Pay Scale

  • According to the information given by the Central Government and as per the 7th pay commission, the teacher's monthly salary is ₹ ​35600- 112800/-.

How to apply for JKSSB Recruitment 2021?

Press the Apply Now button above. You can directly and easily start the application process of the  Jr Assistant, Steno. Fill in your details and submit it or follow all the steps given below.

  •  First, go to the official website of JKSSB and register yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification, log in and then provide the details and documents asked by JKSSB.
  • Then save it and complete the application submission.
  • Then to fully complete the process you have to submit the application fee.
  • And finally, download the application form for future purposes.
