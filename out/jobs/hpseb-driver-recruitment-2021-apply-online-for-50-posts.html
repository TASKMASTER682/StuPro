Name of the post:

  • Driver

Description:

Himachal Pradesh State Electricity Board issued a total of 50 vacancies for the posts of Driver. The online application for these posts under HPSEB shall start from 22-04-2021. The eligible and interested candidates can apply for this online application before 18-05-2021. For further detailed information regarding educational qualifications, age limit, selection procedure, how to apply, etc. for these posts is mentioned below. For more such updates do visit theprograd.com .The ProGrad jobs.

 

Mode of application & application fee:

The application fee for HPSEB Driver Recruitment 2021:

  • Mode of application: Online
  • The application fee for the General is Rs. 400/-
  • The application fee for SC/ST/ Others is Rs. 100/-
  • No application fee for females

Important dates:

  • The Starting date to apply online is 22-04-2021 
  • The Last date to apply online is 18-05-2021 

Educational qualification:

  • You must have the diploma of Matric and Valid Driving Licence with 2 Years Experience to apply for the post of Driver

Age limit criteria:

  • The minimum age required to apply is 18 Years
  • The maximum age required to apply is 45 Years
  • For age relaxation criteria, you can refer to the notification. The link is given below in The ProGrad jobs.

HPSEB Driver Pay Scale:

  • The pay scale as per the 7th pay commission for the post of Driver is  INR 9400-23202/month
  • Check the detailed notification also something has missed.

Detailed vacancies:

The vacancies in detail are given below. Check below that HPSEB issued this notification for which posts.

  • Driver - 50

How to apply for HPSEB Driver Recruitment 2021?

  • First, visit the official website.
  • Register yourself on the portal of HPSEB.
  • Login yourself on the portal.
  • Find the notification of the Driver.
  • Fill up the complete details that are asked in the application form.
  • Upload your photo and signature with the dimensions asked in the instructions.
  • Submit the application fee
  • Finally, download the application form for future needs.
