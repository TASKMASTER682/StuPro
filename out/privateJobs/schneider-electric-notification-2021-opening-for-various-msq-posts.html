Name of the post:

  • Manager Supplier Quality

Description:

Schneider Electric has issued a notification to fill the vacancies of various posts of Manager Supplier Quality. If you are finding jobs in location then feel free to apply online from The ProGrad .The candidates who are interested in the vacancies issued by Schneider Electric can see the detailed information about key skills required, educational qualification, total vacancies, how to apply, roles, and responsibilities are given below. Schneider Electric is dealing with Electrical products. If you are interested then read the details that are given below.

Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  • Written test
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • Responsible for Sub-assembly and Parts of the total plant,
  • Identify day-to-day non-quality factors, conduct or have other departments conduct in-depth analyses, to give an effective CAPA / RCA and monitor the implementation of actions, to ensure non repeatability of complaints at suppliers & sub-contractors place,
  • Identify with our suppliers & sub-contractors potential issues before they occur and implement preventive solutions,
  • Lead quality issue resolution through containment, correction, and prevention steps and check the effectiveness of suppliers’ actions,
  • Conduct SAM and process audits at suppliers & sub-contractors and launch improvement action plans to help them develop their quality performance,
  • Deploy the Quality programs at suppliers & sub-contractors place,
  • Management plan of metrology for Parts Qualification,
  • Work with Cross-Functional Teams and participate in meeting with suppliers, sub-contractors, plant, different Schneider Unit, and different country,
  • Exposure on 5 Why, G8D, Six Sigma methodology...
  • Use Schneider tool and software and support or train our suppliers & sub-contractors for using it,
  • PFMEA, Plan Control management,
  • Collect and prepare monthly report,

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.
