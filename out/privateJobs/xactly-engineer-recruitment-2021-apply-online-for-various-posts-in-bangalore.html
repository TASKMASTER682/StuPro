Selection Process:

Role and responsibility:

  •  Attend New Hire Training
  • Learn the Dev and QE processes
  • Gain exposure to Xactly’s product suite

  • Get to know your team

  • The candidates must have completed the Graduation from a recognized university to apply for the application Xactly Engineer Recruitment 2021

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Bachelor or Masters plus 0-2 year’s experience working on Enterprise application leveraging J2EE and/or AWS.
  • Good knowledge of SQL (preferable working experience with Oracle DB)
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Basic Knowledge and understanding of the different performance, scalability, reliability, and usability as related to the troubleshooting of enterprise applications.
  • Help customers troubleshoot issues they encounter while using the software and provide actionable tips to resolve the problem.
  • Basic knowledge of debugging Java Applications to troubleshoot the Customer issues they encounter while using the different products.
  •  Monitor enablement of various micro-services which will be used by hundreds of thousands of users
  • Basic Knowledge on development of Application with Spring-Boot Microservices Architecture.
