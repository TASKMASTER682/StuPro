Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  • Written test
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • You will be responsible for developing, testing, documenting, and implementing features of Datalink and Tools.

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for the Collins Aerospace job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the BE/BTech from a recognized university to apply for the application Collins Aerospace Engineer Recruitment 2021

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering.
  • Experience in C/C++ and python programming (Hands-on experience).
  • Understanding of ARINC protocols like Arinc661, Arinc429, Arinc618 ..are expected
  • A strong understanding of the Software Development Life Cycle is mandatory.
  • Experience in Requirement Based Testing and System Testing.
  • Knowledge/Understanding in A661 Based Framework is expected.
  • Knowledge of D0-178B is desirable
  • Knowledge of Agile Framework.
  • Knowledge of Collins internal tools like PREP, Doors, SVN, JIRA, Vista, etc. 

Collins Aerospace Associate Software Engineer Payscale:

  • The basic Payscale of an Associate Software Engineer is 80,000/month.

The document needs to be carried:

The candidates who will be selected for the interview must carry the below-mentioned documents:

  • The first and foremost thing is your resume/CV which has your educational background and other related information.
  • You must have your Adhaar card for identity purposes.
  • You must have other identity proofs like a PAN card, voter id, or Passport to prove your citizenship.
  • At last, you should have photocopies of important educational certificates.
