Selection Process:

  • Written-test

Role and responsibility:

  • Work on a team of DFT engineers, working closely with RTL and Physical Designer engineers.
  • Write basic to complex scripts to automate the DFT flow.
  • Develop tests that can be used for Production in the ATE flow.
  • Communicate and work with multi-disciplined and multi-site teams.
  • Work with junior members of the DFT team to deliver overall deliverables for an IP or SoC

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for Google job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the Diploma Or Degree In Engineering from a recognized university to apply for the application Google

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Proficiency with a scripting language (such as Perl), and proficiency with Synthesis, Lint, CDC, LEC and DFT timing and STA.
  • Knowledge of high-performance design DFT techniques.
  • Understanding of the end-to-end flows - Design, Verification, DFT, and PD.
  • Ability to scale DFT, with a focus on minimal area overhead.
