Description:

A new private job vacancy from Visa has been published in Bangalore for the post of Network Support Engineer. If you are looking for a job in Bangalore, then this is an opportunity for you, you must have relevant skills to apply for these Vacancies in Bangalore. If you are interested in these posts which have come out in Bangalore, then check the details below, which include Roles and Responsibility, Key Skills, Documents Required, etc.

Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Role and responsibility:

  • Respond to and resolve IP network issues within the timeframe of severity level agreements
  • Deploy client solutions and network design implementations
  • Support all aspects of the network management process assuring requests, implementation, and problems are delivered as required with no impact on the global communications environment
  • Take initiative to proactively discover potential network impacts
  • Participate in or lead problem resolution and root-cause analysis
  • Participate in tasks related to change, incident, and problem management
  • The document, create and maintain operational run-books based on best practice and from knowledge/experience gained from day-to-day operations for continuous operational improvement.

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Good working knowledge in F5 LTM/GTM, Cisco Catalyst/Nexus switches, Arista switches, Cisco/Juniper routers, Cumulus.
  • Experience or background with automation using Ansible and/or Python would be desired.
