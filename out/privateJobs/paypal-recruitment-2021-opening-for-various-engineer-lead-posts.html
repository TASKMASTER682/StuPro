Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  • Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • This position will be an individual contributor role, focused on guiding a team of 4-5 to provide manual and automated testing of Partner, Marketplace, and Multilateral use cases.
  • As such, it will involve the creation of test plans, scoping the test effort, executing test cases, and frequent interaction with both product and engineering teams. Identifying which features or use cases apply to a given project will be key.

The candidates who are interested to apply for a Paypal  job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the graduation from a recognized university to apply for the application Paypal Recruitment 2021 

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Testing efforts will cover REST APIs, as well as mobile and web UI.
  • Demonstrated expertise in software integration testing is essential.
  • Strong Java skills are required.
  • A proven track record of delivering in a fast-paced, complex environment is required; a minimum of 5 years of relevant experience is expected. Familiarity with TestNg, Jenkins, SauceLabs, Selenium, JIRA, and GitHub is a plus.
