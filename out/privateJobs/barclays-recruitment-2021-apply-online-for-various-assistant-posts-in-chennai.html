Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • Develop a sound understanding of daily processes – “the what”, “the why” and “the how”.
  • Review P&L attributions and prepare commentaries and other MI
  • Maintain trader mandate, monitor Volcker limits.
  • Submit Clean & Hypo P&L for back-testing
  • Attend to ad hoc queries from the traders and other infrastructure partners relating to trading revenue and Balance Sheet.
  • Weekly / Monthly Responsibilities
  • Balance Sheet control and commentary, Month End adjustments, and Balance Sheet Substantiation
  • Perform key weekly & monthly reporting to senior management.
  • Attend to queries from internal and external audits and reviews and maintain keystroke documentations for key PC processes, risk assessment, and SOX documentation.

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for the Barclays job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the CFA / Masters in Finance from a recognized university to apply for the application Barclays Recruitment 2021

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Experienced with MS office toolkit (Word, PPT, Excel, Access Database etc)
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills in both formal and informal settings & ability to interact with the region and UK / US as part of a global team (written and verbal) Strong control awareness – in particular, the ability to identify and escalate potential control breakdowns and to streamline processes in an effective and efficient manner.
  • Has the initiative and ability to break down problems into components parts and resolving them
  • Attention to detail and analytical
  • Confident and assertive manner
