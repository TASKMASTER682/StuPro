Name of the post:

  • Layout Design Engineer

Description:

Synopsys has issued a notification to fill the vacancies of various posts of Layout Design Engineer. If you are finding jobs in Bangalore then feel free to apply online from The ProGrad .The candidates who are interested in the vacancies issued by Synopsys can see the detailed information about key skills required, educational qualification, total vacancies, how to apply, roles, and responsibilities are given below. Synopsys is dealing with electronic products. If you are interested then read the details that are given below.

Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  •  Written-test
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  •  High-Speed DDR/LPDDR I/O Layout design including GPIO and Special IO’s.
  • Work with DDR PHY team, package engineers, and system engineers to meet design specs.
  • Perform scheduling duties, Remote site interaction, etc.
  • Work with the local team to support the critical layout and floorplanning requirements
  • Coordination duties with other layout teams both in Bangalore and globally, to detail out layout activities and obtain layout deliverables. This includes reviewing and quality checking from remote Layout teams.
  • Strict flow adherence and policing of internal policies to secure schedules.

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for the Synopsys job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the B.Tech, M.Tech from a recognized university to apply for the application Synopsys Engineer Recruitment 2021

Key skills required :

  • Experience in Analog Mixed-signal layout and verification of high-speed digital and/or DDR IOs, Critical signal inclusion.
  • Advanced understanding of Deep submicron effects and mitigation, Advanced tool usage, Advanced floor planning techniques, understand the digital flow, Advanced strategies.
  • Solid understanding of CMOS and FinFET layouts and process technology in 28nm and smaller.
  • The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:
  • Good understanding of IO frame and pitch requirements, power rail routings, IO abutment rules and requirements, bond pad layout, EM and IR considerations, DFM, etc.
  • Scripting skills for layout automation is a plus
  • Remote site interaction, layout coordination activities, ability to foster accountability and ownership through hands-on technical leadership.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in interactions with customers, and internal development teams.
