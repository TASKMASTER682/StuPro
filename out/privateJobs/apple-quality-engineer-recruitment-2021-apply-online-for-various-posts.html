Name of the post:

  • Quality Engineer

Description:

Apple has issued a notification to fill the vacancies of various posts of Quality Engineer. If you are finding jobs in Bangalore then feel free to apply online from The ProGrad .The candidates who are interested in the vacancies issued by Apple can see the detailed information about key skills required, educational qualification, total vacancies, how to apply, roles, and responsibilities are given below. Apple is dealing with electronics gadgets. If you are interested then read the details that are given below.

Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  •  Written test
  • Group Discussion
  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • Coordinate all Quality Engineering functions for a program, serving as a project manager to lead efforts at supplier locations
  • Establish audit procedures appropriate to program and supplier to ensure consistency to Apple quality standards
  • Evaluate and communicate yields of new and existing processes
  • Drive corrective actions multi-functionally with partners
  • Travel internationally to supplier sites – 25%

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for the Apple  job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the passed B.E, B.Tech, M.S from a recognized university to apply for the application.

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Proven experience in manufacturing and Quality Control.
  • Experience developing process quality plans, supplier quality audits, and driving corrective actions and failure analysis efforts.
  • Solid understanding of mechanical quality and reliability concepts, supplier management, and general manufacturing operations.
  • Experience with the development and evaluation of measurement systems
  • Project management experience and a deep understanding of problem-solving tools including design of experiments, root cause analysis, and statistical analytical tools
  • Experience with any of the following: mechanical enclosures, castings, plastic parts, injection molding, tooling, jigs, fixtures, or automotive high volume quality manufacturing engineering

Payscale info. :

  • The Payscale information will be decided at the time of the interview.

The document needs to be carried:

  • You must have your Adhaar card for identity purposes.
  • You must have other identity proofs like a PAN card, voter id, or Passport to prove your citizenship.
  • At last, you should have photocopies of important educational certificates.
