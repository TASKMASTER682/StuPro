Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies has released.

Selection Process:

  • Written test

Role and responsibility:

  • Read, Interpret & classify released engineering changes
  • Responsible for physical implementation of ECO's at Suppliers/Factories globally
  • Technical support to Suppliers / Factories on change implementation
  • Responsible to validate new design manufacturability with supplier & enrich purchase info record
  • RFQ - secure that missing master data is enriched ( Info records, source lists and quota arrangements) within agreed SLA

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for Vestas job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the Degree, B.E from a recognized university to apply for the application Vestas RFQ Engineer Recruitment 2021 

Key skills required :

The candidates who are interested to apply in these job openings must have the following key skills that are required to do this:

  • Ability to read, understand and communicate Engineering drawings and Technical specifications
  • Exposure in handling production documentation
  • Experience with selection, qualification, negotiation and contracting with suppliers
  • Experience in supplier consolidation and cost out projects
  • Fluent in English. (Oral & Written
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel & Power point for reporting day to day developments/ reporting
