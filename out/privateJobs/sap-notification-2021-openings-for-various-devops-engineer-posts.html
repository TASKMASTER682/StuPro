Name of the post:

  • Associate Business Process Consultant

Description:

SAP has issued a notification to fill the vacancies of Associate Business Process Consultant. If you are finding jobs in Haryana then feel free to apply online from The ProGrad .The candidates who are interested in the vacancies issued by SAP can see the detailed information about key skills required, educational qualification, total vacancies, how to apply, roles, and responsibilities are given below. SAP is dealing with software products. If you are interested then read the details that are given below.

Total vacancies:

  • Various vacancies have released.

Selection Process:

  • Personal Interview

Role and responsibility:

  • SAP consulting experience including customer-facing implementations, rollouts & expert advisory assignments in the S/4HANA environment.
  • The candidate would be expected to independently work as an expert in the financials consulting domain.
  • Ability to lead a team of consultants and underwrite solution delivery without escalations
  • The candidate would be expected to participate in customer workshops and manage scope and customer expectations

Application fee:

  • No information is given about the application required.

Educational qualification:

The candidates who are interested to apply for SAP job must have the following qualification:

  • The candidates must have completed the B.Tech/ M.Tech/ MCA/ M.Sc in Computer Science from a recognized university to apply for the application SAP Notification 2021

Key skills required :

  • Professionally qualified Chartered Accountant/CMA/MBA Finance with 3-4 years of SAP consulting experience in Finance & Controlling besides a good understanding of business processes obtained during previous stints with established corporate /industry/ audit firms /consulting firms.
  • Must have an understanding of FICO processes covered or related to AR, AP, GL, AA, IO, CCA, Integration, tax processes etc.
  • Hands-on experience in any ONE of the following areas of Financial Supply Chain Management (FSCM – Credit Management, Collections/Disputes), Treasury, Cash Management (Cash Flows, Inhouse Cash), FICA and Advanced Product costing, Advanced Profitability, Financial Consolidation/Reporting, Central Finance and IFRS 15/16 know-how would be an added advantage
  • Good understanding of the functional integration aspects with other SAP modules
  • Must have experience in S/4HANA greenfield implementations or conversions
  • Should have 2 or more, end to end Process Implementation cycles experience.
  • The candidate should have excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Ability to work well in/with teams and build long-term customer relationships.
  • Certification in the Core area
  • Should be Travel-ready and able to work with customers/teams across cultures

SAP DevOps Engineer Payscale:

  • The Payscale information will be decided at the time of the interview.

The document needs to be carried:

The candidates who will be selected for the interview must carry the below-mentioned documents:

  • The first and foremost thing is your resume/CV which has your educational background and other related information.
  • You must have your Adhaar card for identity purposes.
  • You must have other identity proofs like a PAN card, voter id, or Passport to prove your citizenship.
  • At last, you should have photocopies of important educational certificates.

  • Note: You can also apply by clicking the apply now button shown above to apply directly from here
  • First of all, read the SAP Notification 2021 notification thoroughly at the official website @sap.com.
  • You should have an email id and a phone number for communication purposes.
  • Click on the apply now link for the post you want to apply for.
  • Fill in the required details and submit.

  • Finally submit and download the application form for future purposes.
